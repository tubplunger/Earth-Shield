# Earth-Shield
An Asteroid style game where you are the last line of defense for Earth. 

This game is similar to the game Asteroid and has a similar premise: you control a ship where you have to dodge and destroy asteroids that appear from the sides of the screen.

The ship is meant to control well though when the player goes to stop there is a bit of inertia that remains until it comes to a complete stop. Rotating the ship will be separate from this movement and it can only fire from the front.

The asteroids will come in different sizes with different health maximums, with some taking a short amount of time to destroy and some taking longer. Again, depends on their size.

For scoring, each asteroid will give points when they are destroyed with the player gathering these points until they take enough hits from the asteroids to be destroyed themselves. The game then loops from there.
